QuoteSystemX

Orchestrated quoting pipelines with guardrails.

QuoteSystemX ingests complex RFQs, runs pricing models, and publishes composed offers. Operators monitor every stage through a mission control experience patterned after modern HCI dashboards.

  • Composable pricing engines with versioned strategies.
  • Live health map for integrations and SLA triggers.
  • “Moments” stream to replay decisions for audits.
  • Policy packs that codify procurement playbooks.

RecipientOFQuotes

Post-delivery telemetry meets relationship analytics.

RecipientOFQuotes extends the lifecycle after quotes leave the building. Each recipient channel—email, partner portal, or API webhook—feeds engagement signals back to QuoteSystemX for instant iteration.

  • Adaptive layouts optimized for every recipient persona.
  • Feedback capsules capture sentiment directly inside quotes.
  • Engagement scoring loops into forecast accuracy.
  • Auto-escalation for stalled or at-risk conversations.

Engagement

RecipientOFQuotes renders tailored experiences, streaming telemetry into a unified lake for analytics, alerts, and AI copilots.
